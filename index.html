Section 01

Overview

This is some of the basic skills, duties, and responsibilities required for AI engineering.

Basic Duties & Responsibilities

According to WorkBC, the basic responsibilities of an AI Engineer include:

  • Building AI systems and integrating it into businesses or corporations for commercial use

  • Designing Algorithms and optimizing them for performance and scalability

  • Manipulating, processing, and analyzing large datasets to train AI models

  • Deploying AI technologies into API's, establishing connections with other devices and programs

  • Evaluating Performance of AI systems and models and making improvements based on the results

  • Collaborating with different groups to understand market needs and to make AI solutions for them.

Basic Requirements

For education, you need at least a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or some related field.

Some technical skills you should have are in programming languages like Python and C++, and frameworks like the two major ones, TensorFlow and PyTorch, maybe even learn Mastra if your really good.

For time commitment, you should be prepared to work full time and stay updated with the latest advancements in the AI field. This job basicly takes over your entire life and still requires more.

Section 02

Market View

This is the salary potential and job growth predictions for AI Engineers in British Columbia.

Average Salary

127,558

The average salary for AI Engineers in British Columbia is $127,558 per year. This number can actually vary a lot depending of experience and other factors. For example an entry level AI Engineer earns on avarage at $89,123, while a senior engineer could earn over $145,600+ depending on work and projects.

BC Job Growth Forecast

4.2% per year

In the next ten years, acording to the BC labor market outlook, the province expects significant growth in the sub category of computer system design, with 1.05 million new job openings. Since the government is investing in AI research and development, the demand for skilled AI Engineers is expected to continue growing.
